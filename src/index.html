About me

Hello! I’m Sander Molenkamp, a cloud software architect and Microsoft Azure MVP with over 20 years of professional experience. I work for Info Support where I design and develop customer solutions using cloud native architectures.

Public speaking

Whenever I have the opportunity, I love to speak at conferences and user groups. Here are some samples of past and future scheduled events:

dotnetFlix

dotnetFlix is a video-channel for developers. In each episode we try to inform and inspire people in the area of software-development with Microsoft technology, often featuring a guest from the community. This is not limited to writing software only, but also about all the other challenges we face as developers (cloud, containers, AI, IoT, DevOps etc.).
